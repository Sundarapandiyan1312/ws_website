Detection Systems

Advanced detection systems including VHF radio direction finders, GNSS interference detection, C-UAS systems, and jamming.

IZT R5509 VHF Radio Direction Finder

The IZT R5509 is a highly accurate and reliable radio direction finding (DF) system designed for high resolution VHF direction estimation.

OHB - GIDAS

GIDAS (GNSS Interference Detection and Analysis System): a specialized, high-performance system for detecting, classifying, and localizing jamming and spoofing signals to protect GNSS (GPS, Galileo, etc.) services.

OHB - NavTD M23

NavTD M23 is able to generate and transmit jamming and spoofing signals to test the effect of navigation warfare and the capabilities of countermeasures in a real environment.

Eclipse – C-UAS system

The C-UAS system Eclipse detects and disrupts drones across all known FPV frequency ranges, offering enhanced protection for artillery, troops and other military vehicles and installations.

Oscilion - Astor IV

Oscilion has launched the ITAR-free ASTOR IV jamming system, designed for operator training and to counter modern jamming waveforms across the full EM radar spectrum.
